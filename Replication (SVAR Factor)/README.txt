Code that replicates the results in 
Korobilis, Dimitris (2022), "A new algorithm for structural restrictions in vector autoregressions". 
https://papers.ssrn.com/sol3/papers.cfm?abstract_id=3557911, forthcoming in European Economic Review

There are three main folders:

1) MONTE CARLO: Replicates results of the Monte Carlo exercise of Section 3
2) EMPIRICAL_PART_A: Replicates results of the empirical exercise of Section 4.1 (baseline model)
3) EMPIRICAL_PART_B: Replicates results of the empirical exercise of Section 4.2 (extended model)

There are comments at the beginning of each m-file to direct you as to which figures/tables can be replicated
using each file. Here is a summary:

 1) ~\MONTE_CARLO\MONTE_CARLO.m:
     Replicates Figure 1 and Table 2 of Section 3.1, as well as Figures C1-C4 of the online supplement

 2) ~\MONTE_CARLO\MONTE_CARLO_TIMES.m:
     Replicates Table 3 of Section 3.2

 3) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_A\BVAR_FSR.m:
     Replicates Figure 2 of Section 4.1, as well as Figures C6-C9 in the online supplement

 4) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_A\BVAR_FSR_n_r.m:
     Replicates panel (b) of Figure 3 of Section 4.1

 5) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_A\BVAR_FSR_1plus4shocks.m:
     Replicates panel (c) of Figure 3 of Section 4.1

 6) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_A\BVAR_FSR_1shock.m:
     Replicates panel (d) of Figure 3 of Section 4.1

 7) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_A\BVAR_FSR_noninf.m:
     Replicates Figure C10 of Section C.3.1 of the online supplement

 8) ~\EMPIRICAL_PART_B\BVAR_FSR.m:
     Replicates Figure 4 of Section 4.2, as well as Figures C11-C16 of the online supplement
